About

Description:

For my senior capstone project (completed as a junior), I was part of a team that created an internal-facing data dashboard that allows Court Watch Nola, a New Orleans-based non-profit, to efficiently query their data and create customizable visualizations.

Court Watch NOLA (CWN) is a non-profit organization located in New Orleans, Louisiana that is "dedicated to promoting transparency, equity, and justice through tracking, observing, and reporting thousands of criminal court cases a year." CWN sends well-trained observers into court sessions of all three Orleans Parish court systems (Magistrate, Criminal District Court, and Municipal). These observers fill out court-specific surveys, which are then uploaded to a popular survey data collection website.

Awards/Recognitions:

1st Place Crowd Favorite, 2022 Tulane School of Science and Engineering Activities Expo (SSE Expo)--a yearly venue in which all capstone groups show off their projects.

Description:

For my Introduction to Data Science class (Fall 2021 semester), my partner and I conducted a semester-long project analyzing over 10,000 publicly available Orleans Parish court dockets. Ultimately, we discovered possible biases in the Orleans Parish court system, such as 400-500% higher bond prices for Black people for several charges (when compared against other races).

Description:

For my Software Studio class (Fall 2021), our semester long project was to build a website using Ruby on Rails. My group built DreamCatcher, a website that allows people to input and track their dreams, as well as explore others' dreams.

DreamCatcher is a complete SQL Database-backed website that allows for profile creation and login/logout functionality. Once a user has created their profile, they can enter new dreams from the "New Dream" page; here, they can mark the dream as "Public" or "Private." All dreams marked public are shown on the "Explore" page, making DreamCatcher a dream social media of sorts. DreamCatcher also supports searching--users can enter a phrase in the search bar to search for all public dreams matching their search phrase.
